Gaming/Computing

I have a keen interest in gaming.

This year I undertook a project to build my own gaming computer from scrath. I had no prior knowledge in how to build computers so I had to do a lot of research prior to the build process.

Once built I was extremely happy with the outcome. I found the whole process thoroughly enjoyable and a great learning experience.

My favourite game is Fallout 3. The game takes place in the year 2277, 200 years after the Great War, on the East Coast of what used to be the United States, mostly in Washington, D.C., Southwest Maryland, Western Pennsylvania and Northeast Virginia. The gameplay features include real-time combat and first or third person perspective.
